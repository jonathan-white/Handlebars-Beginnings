Beginnings

Since its inception, Akron, Ohio, has been a community of beginnings — many of which are highlighted throughout this book.

In that same point, so too has the Junior League of Akron been an organization of beginnings, developing and donating numerous charitable projects to benefit the hometown we love so well.

What better way to express our enthusiasm and continued support than by offering this special collection of appetizers, the beginnings of so many meals and gatherings, to all of you.

Profits from the purchase of this cookbook will benefit the continued charitable effortsof the Junior League of Akron and its projects.
