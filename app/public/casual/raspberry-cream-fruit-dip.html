Raspberry Cream Fruit Dip

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 cup raspberries, crushed
  • Assorted fresh fruit

Beat the cream cheese, brown sugar, wine winegar and ginger in a mixing bowl until blended, scraping the bowl occasionally. Stir in the raspberries. Spoon into a serving bowl. Chill, covered, until serving time. Serve with fresh fruit. Yield: 1 1/4 cups.
