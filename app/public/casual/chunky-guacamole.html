Chunky Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe avocados
  • 1 large ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • Tortilla chips

Mash the avocados in a bowl until of a chunky consistency. Stir in the tomato, onion, lemon juice, salt and white pepper. Serve with tortilla chips. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
